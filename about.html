About Us

Exploring Sri Lanka is an epic adventure, with its diverse landscapes, magnificent wildlife, fabulous food, ancient cities, thrilling history and unique culture, it is like no other tropical island in the world.

After travelling across the island and being inspired by its diversity and natural beauty we wanted to share the story of our journey so that it could become a useful source of reference for other travellers when planning their own itinerary for visiting the island.

Based in England with a passion for travel, we are not travel journalists or part of the travel industry. The articles, features, travel-tips and reviews we publish are based on our own personal observations and experience.

Whether you’re looking for a luxury travel destination, a backpacking holiday or anything in between, Sri Lanka seems to have something for everyone. We highlight the best of this extraordinary island nation to inspire travellers who go in search of authentic, real life experiences in exciting destinations around the world.
